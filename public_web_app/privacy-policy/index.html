Privacy Policy

How We Use Information

Third-Party Services

Data Security

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting samanyudu@gmail.com . Account deletion requests will be processed within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our app and website are not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal, operational, or product changes. Updated versions will be posted on this page with immediate effect.
