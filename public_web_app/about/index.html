About Us

Samanyudu TV is an independent regional digital news platform dedicated to public-interest journalism in Andhra Pradesh, India.

We publish original Telugu news content across politics, local governance, public services, economy, and sports with a professional and evidence-based editorial approach.

Our content is managed through an internal editorial dashboard, where submissions are screened, verified, and processed through review checkpoints before publishing.

Every article undergoes editorial review for factual accuracy, language quality, and relevance before it is made available to readers on web and mobile platforms.

Mission: Deliver accurate, timely, and unbiased regional news that supports informed public discussion.

Location: Andhra Pradesh, India.
